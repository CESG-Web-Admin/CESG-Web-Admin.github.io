**Call for Papers**
{: style="font-size: 125%; text-align: center;"}

The 41st CESG Conference is organized by the Department of Economics at Simon Fraser University in Vancouver, British Columbia, from October 16-18, 2026.

The conference theme is **"Recent Advances in Econometrics: robustness, flexibility and adaptivity"** The keynote speakers are: 

**Yoosoon Chang (Indiana U.)**
{: style="text-align: center;"}
**Tong Li (Vanderbilt U.)** 
{: style="text-align: center;"}
**Pedro Sant’Anna (Emory U.)**
{: style="text-align: center;"}


**Contributed papers for presentation at the conference are now invited.** Contributions are welcome in theoretical, applied, or computation econometrics. Submissions will be considered for regular and poster sessions, and only completed papers (submitted in PDF format) will be considered.

Prospective contributors are invited to submit papers filling the submission 
form available <a href="https://forms.gle/ANKCAzXdkViM9FCt7" target="_blank">here</a> until June 30th, 2026. 

Notification of acceptance or rejection will be made by mid-July 2026. 
All attendees are expected to register for the conference. 

For up-to-date information on the conference, please visit [https://caneconometrics.ca](https://caneconometrics.ca). 
 
 Local organizers: Bertille Antoine, Dongwoo Kim, Xiaoting Sun.
